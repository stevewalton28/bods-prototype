Submit bus service data to BODS

This service helps small bus operators to submit their data to the Bus Open Data Service (BODS) by simplifying the process of creating TransXChange XML files.

The Bus Open Data Service (BODS) requires all bus operators to publish information about their routes, timetables, and fares to a central digital service.

Using this service, you will:

  1. Enter your operator details
  2. Provide service and route information
  3. Add bus stop details
  4. Enter journey and timetable information
  5. Review your submission
  6. Download a valid TransXChange XML file

The service will validate your information against the TransXChange schema to ensure it meets the requirements for BODS submission.

What you'll need

Before you start, make sure you have:

  • your operator licence details
  • information about your service, including service code
  • details of all bus stops on your route (ATCO codes if available)
  • journey timings and frequency information

This process should take approximately 15-30 minutes to complete, depending on the complexity of your service.
